Risk register — pilot day edition

Three categories: risks that could break the pilot today (P0), risks that will surface within the first week (P1), and known gaps that must be closed in v0.1.5 (P2).

R-1
Offer L3 body data is illustrative — pilots may misread it as real The Remuneration, Contract, and E-Signature steps in Offer-Candidate.jsx display offerContractMock data (Rp 32.000.000, "Dewi Sartika", etc.). A pilot recruiter opening an offer for a real candidate sees fake compensation numbers. This could cause confusion or distrust. Mitigation: Add a prominent yellow banner to Offer-Candidate.jsx steps: "Step data is illustrative — full compensation entry launches in v0.1.5." Brief pilot users verbally and in the onboarding deck.
P0 · TODAY
R-2
Onboarding backend is 0% — pilot sees fake candidates Pilots who advance a hire to Onboarding will see the Pre-Boarding/Day-1-30/Probation UI but with fake data from onboardingMock (candidate "Bagas Pratama", start date "15 Apr", etc.). Mitigation: Add a "Feature Preview" badge to the Onboarding page header. Brief pilot users. Priority for task 12.1 in v0.1.5.
P0 · TODAY

Compensation engine accuracy — untested against DJP official data The PPh21/BPJS engine (201 lines) has not been unit-tested against official DJP examples. If any bracket boundary, PTKP value, or BPJS cap is wrong, every compensation calculation is wrong. Not a pilot-blocker today (step uses mock data), but must be fixed before Offer L3 wire-up in v0.1.5. Mitigation: Task 12.2 — week 1 of v0.1.5.
