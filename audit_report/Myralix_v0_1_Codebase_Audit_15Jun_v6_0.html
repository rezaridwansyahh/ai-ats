9.1 Glossary for non-technical readers

ATSApplicant Tracking System — the software a recruiter uses to manage hiring end-to-end.
Multi-tenancySupporting multiple separate companies on one install without them seeing each other's data.
Interview moduleThe third stage of the recruitment funnel. Covers scheduling sessions with candidates, conducting competency-based interviews, scoring with rubric, and deciding whether to advance.
RubricA scoring framework with weighted competencies. The Interview rubric uses HRD-01 to HRD-06 (Leadership, Planning, Problem Solving, Quality, Creativity, Teamwork), each rated 1–7.
4-Level WorkspaceL1 Workboard · L2 Position · L3 Candidate · L4 Calibration. The pattern every module follows.
checkBudgetOrThrow()Backend function that checks monthly AI spend against the company's budget cap. Throws 402 (Payment Required) if exceeded. Fires a Slack alert at 80% usage.
compensation_engineBackend service that computes Indonesian salary rollup: PPh21 + PTKP + BPJS Kesehatan + BPJS Ketenagakerjaan + Gross/Nett toggle. The most technically complex piece remaining.
UU PDPIndonesia's data privacy law (UU 27/2022). BG Check must collect explicit candidate consent before any verification.
UU 13/2003 §60Indonesian labour law governing probation period and the Day-90 onboarding decision (Konversi/Perpanjangan/Pemutusan).
PKWTT / PKWTIndonesian permanent (PKWTT) vs fixed-term (PKWT) labour contracts. The Day-90 Onboarding decision chooses between them.
